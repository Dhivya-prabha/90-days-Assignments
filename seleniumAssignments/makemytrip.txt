1) Go to https://www.makemytrip.com/
2) Click Hotels
3) Enter city as Goa, and choose Goa, India
4) Enter Check in date as May 15 and Check out as May 30
5) Click on ROOMS & GUESTS and click 2 Adults and one Children. Click Apply Button.
6) Click Search button
7) Select 5 start in Star Category under Select Filters
8) Click Show More under Facility and select Restaurant and Wi-Fi
9) Click on the first resulting hotle and go to the new window
10) Click MORE OPTIONS link and Select 3Months plan.
11) Print the Plan of lowest Annual Interset and click close
12) Print the Tariff Inclusions
13) Print the resort name and check with the page title.
14) Close the browser
