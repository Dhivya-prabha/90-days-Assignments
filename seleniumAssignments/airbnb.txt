1) Go to https://www.airbnb.co.in/
2) Type Goa in location and select Goa Beach, Baga
3) Select 2 Adults, one kid in Guest field and  click search
4) Select Hotel Room as type of place and Price as minimum 1000 , maximum as 10000
5) Click More filters add bed as 2, bedroom as one and click show stays
6) Click the first result and go to the new window
7) Capture the Price for one night
8) Under Select check-in date, choose check in date after 45 days from now and checkout date as 5 days after
9) Check your chosen date is selected above reserve button
10) Calculate the one night price x 5 days which is displayed in the page is match
11)  Close the browser.
